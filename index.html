Story & Objective

I started building software out of necessity. While managing my analyst job alongside a master's degree, the workload became too much to handle with standard tools.

What began as a way to automate tasks quickly evolved. With the help of AI, I found I could create truly innovative tools. The challenge of solving a difficult problem with a clever piece of software was energizing. It wasn't just about efficiency; it was about the excitement of building something new and effective. This passion for building became my primary motivation, and I began helping friends with their own unique challenges.

That experience set my career path. What I learned by necessity is now my profession. This portfolio is the evidence of what I can build, and I'm ready to bring that same direct, problem-solving approach to a full-time developer role.
